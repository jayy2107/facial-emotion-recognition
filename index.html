// System Architecture

How It
Works

01
Webcam Capture
OpenCV captures 30+ frames per second from the MacBook camera continuously
02
Grayscale Convert
Frames converted to black & white for faster and more precise processing
03
Face Detection
Haar Cascade XML model scans each frame and locates faces with bounding boxes
04
AI Analysis
DeepFace neural network analyzes facial landmarks and predicts dominant emotion
05
Live Display
Emotion label and bounding box rendered on screen in real time at 30+ FPS
